Welcome

This website is my project space for LIS 500. I use it to share reflections and resources about how technology is shaped by power, and how bias can show up in code, data, and everyday digital tools.

Why this topic matters

When technology is treated as neutral, it becomes easier to ignore who it helps, who it harms, and who it leaves out. My goal is to learn how to notice hidden assumptions and make more responsible choices as a student and future professional.

Where to go next

Use the navigation links at the top to move between pages. The implicit bias page includes beginner friendly explanations and outside resources, and the tech hero page profiles a person working to make tech more equitable.
