Welcome

Welcome to my website for LIS 500: Code and Power. This site will be used to share course materials, reflections, and resources as we explore how technology and information systems are shaped by power, bias, and social structures.

Throughout the course, I examine how assumptions about race, gender, class, and access become embedded in technology and what that means for equity and responsibility in the digital world.

This website includes an About Me page, resources for understanding implicit bias and intersectionality, and a Tech Hero profile highlighting someone working to improve equity in the technology space.
