Welcome

Welcome to my page for LIS 500: Code and Power. This site will share course materials, reflections, and resources as we explore how technology and code are shaped by power and societal bias.

Throughout the course, I’ll examine how assumptions about race, gender, class, and access become embedded in information systems—and what that means for equity, responsibility, and everyday life online.
