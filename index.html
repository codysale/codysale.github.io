Welcome

Welcome to my website for LIS 500: Code and Power. This site shares course materials, reflections, and resources exploring how technology and information systems are shaped by power, bias, and social inequality.

Throughout this course, I examine how assumptions about race, gender, class, and access become embedded in technology and what that means for fairness, responsibility, and equity in the digital world.

This website includes an About Me page, a resource guide on implicit bias and intersectionality, and a Tech Hero profile highlighting someone working to improve equity in technology.

Created by: Cody Sale
