Hi, I am Cody

I am a student interested in how technology connects to real life outcomes, especially in areas like money, opportunity, and access. In this class I want to understand how bias can enter systems through design choices, data, and policies.

My goal with this site is to build a clean multi page project that shows what I learned, while also practicing clear writing, accessibility basics, and organized code.

What I am focusing on

  • How bias can be built into systems without obvious intent
  • How intersectionality changes the way people experience technology
  • How to design and communicate in more inclusive ways
