Hi! My name is Cody. I’m a student at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, and I’m interested in finance, technology, and how online systems shape real-life opportunities.

In LIS 500, I’m focusing on how bias can show up in everyday tools (like search results, hiring platforms, and algorithms) and how understanding intersectionality can help us design fairer systems.
