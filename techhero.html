Tech Hero

Timnit Gebru

Timnit Gebru is a computer scientist known for her work on algorithmic bias and ethical AI. She helped push the tech industry to take fairness seriously by highlighting how datasets and systems can reinforce discrimination.

Gebru co-founded the Distributed AI Research Institute (DAIR), which focuses on responsible AI research that centers real-world harms and community impact.

Why she challenges norms: She advocates for accountability in AI—especially when technology affects housing, hiring, policing, and access to opportunity.
